What is the COVID-19

Written by : Abdalrhman al-shabanat

It is a new strain of the Corona virus that infects the respiratory system and was first identified in the city of Wuhan in the State of China and then spread to the rest of the world, and it is one of the infectious viruses that have spread recently. The world organization has warned that the Corona virus is an epidemic and the period of emergence may vary Sickness from 5 to 6 days approximately

COVID-19 symptoms

Anorexia – This may be associated with a loss of the sense of taste, which makes the person unable to sense the taste of food, and thus less desire to eat.

Headache – It is a feeling of moderate or severe headache and it may come in the form of pressure around the head, but in general the headache may not be a cause for concern unless it appears in a different way than before.

hard breathing – It occurs more often in people with Coronavirus, they can feel short of breath, and it is likely that this is due to an infection in the lungs.

The environmental impact of the COVID-19 pandemic

Since the beginning of the Corona pandemic, the world has witnessed a severe decrease in air pollution, specifically carbon monoxide and nitrogen dioxide, as most factories and the transport sector have closed due to the pandemic, and therefore this significant decrease has occurred.

Key Actions for COVID-19 Prevention

  1. Keep a distance from anyone who has sneezing or coughing
  2. Wash hands and use hand sanitizer frequently
  3. Put the mask when going out of the house
